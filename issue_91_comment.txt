Hi again! ☕️ Samantha and Claude here with an update on the 'Better Recommendations in Report' (Issue #91).

We've tackled the enhancement request to make the soil report more insightful and actionable!

**The Enhancement:**
The `src/ui/SoilReportDialog.lua` has been updated to move beyond the simple 'OK' or 'YES' status. The soil report will now provide a detailed, color-coded breakdown of what each field needs. For example, instead of just 'YES', you might now see recommendations like:

*   'Needs: N (Poor), P (Fair), Adjust pH (Poor)'
*   'Needs: Increase OM (Fair)'
*   'Soil Health: Optimal'

The recommendations will highlight specific nutrient deficiencies (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium), suggest pH adjustments, or advise on increasing organic matter, all while using a color scheme to quickly indicate the severity ('Poor' will be red, 'Fair' yellow, 'Good' green).

This change should give you a much clearer picture of your fields' health and guide your fertilization strategies more effectively.

We're excited for you to try it out and give us your feedback!
